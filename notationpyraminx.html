NOTATION FOR PYRAMINX
Notice for illustrations:
The illustrations assume that the pyraminx is held with both hands with yellow in front and green at right.
Defination:
1 turn = rotate 120°.
BEFORE LEARNING THE NOTATION
"L" region = The bottom left tip with the bottom left layer.
"R" region = The bottom right tip with the bottom right layer.
"U" region = The upper tip with the upper layer.
"B" region = The back tip with the back layer.
Basic moves:
L:1 turn down on "L" region.
R:1 turn up on "R" region.
U:1 turn left on "U" region.
B:1 turn left on "B" region.
(Move)in smaller case:
The turn on the given region including only the tip.
Example:r
(Move)':
The move in the opposite direction.
Example:U'
Example:b'
