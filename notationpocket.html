NOTATION FOR POCKET CUBE
Notice for illustrations:
The illustrations assume that the pocket cube is held with both hands with yellow face on top and red face in front.
Definations:
1 turn = rotate 90°
&
2 turns = rotate 180°
Whole layer moves:
L:1 turn down on left layer.
R:1 turn up on right layer.
U:1 turn left on top layer.
D:1 turn right on bottom layer.
F:1 turn down on front layer from right side.
B:1 turn down on back layer from left side.
Rotations:
x:rotate up to the top.
(Same direction as R)
y:rotate left to the left.
(Same direction as U)
z:rotate down to the right.
(Same direction as F)
(Move)':
The move in the opposite direction.
Example:D'
(Move)2:
The same move two times.
Example:B2
Example:B2'
Conclusion:(Move)2 is the same as (MOVE)2'
